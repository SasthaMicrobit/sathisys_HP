About us

Our Company

Sathi Systems Pvt Ltd is a Japan based software company, which has skills and expertise to facilitate complex business solutions. We offer services of entire software, web development, Content Management Solutions and Creative Design from the very beginning until the end.

We have a squad of experts, who are working on modern technologies and tools with vast experience to carry out web based projects in JAVA, DotNET, PHP, CakePHP and programming scripting languages like PHP, Mysql, Jquery, Ajax, CSS, XHTML.

History

Sathi Systems Pvt Ltd, a Japan based software company located at Tirunelveli.This company was started in the year 2009 with the name "Indo-Nikko". And later on in the year 2011 it changed to "Sathi Systems Pvt Ltd". Now currently operating at Perumalpuram, Tirunelveli, Tamilnadu

Our Main Customer is located in Japan named Microbit (www.microbit.co.jp). Microbit is a software company, which has, tie-ups with several major IT companies in Japan.
